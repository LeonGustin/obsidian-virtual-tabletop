  This list is not exhaustive. The players are free to
come up with reasons why they already know each
other and work together. Additionally, there are
three Adventure Hooks in the next section.

   You are free to disregard this if your players are
experienced role players. These instructions are
designed to kickstart the game from the start and
help new players get a hold of the group faster. A
group of experienced players can meet each other
and come up with reasons to work together on the
fly.


Resting and Healing
Eberron and Sharn are places of action. Adven-
tures here tend to be of a faster-pace than in other
settings. If you want to further convey this feeling of
urgency and fast-paced action, consider using the
rules for Epic Heroism (DMG, p267). Short rests
are reduced to 5 minutes and long rests to 1 hour,
with some limitations for spellcasters. This rule is
optional.

following Adventure Hooks.

Looking for Work
The adventurers begin hanging out in House Dene-

only mercenaries for hire. For this, they can lodge
at House Deneith’s enclave, as long as they keep
taking bounties. They follow Professor Daela’s

     Wondrous Item, uncommon

     This object appears to be a 5-inches in diameter
