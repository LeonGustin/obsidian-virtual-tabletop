# Abraxis Wren of Eberron

   Welcome to a world of swashbuckling
action and dark fantasy, a world ruled by
   great dynasties and tempered by war.
     Eberron holds many wonders, from
      dragonmarks to warforges, deadly
        dungeons to elemental airships.
              As the shadows of evil and
                  conspiracy threaten to
             envelop the land, heroes of
                 prophecy come forth to
                            save the day.


                  Collects Infestation 2:
              Dungeons & Dragons, 2012
                   Annual: Eberron, and
                Eberron: Eye of the Wolf .
