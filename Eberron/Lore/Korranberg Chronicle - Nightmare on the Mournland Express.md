armies of the Five Nations did not often have time to

typically combat oriented wizard or sorcerer
cantrips—and one 1st-level spell. Unlike fully trained
wizards or gifted sorcerers, a wandslinger can only
fulfill the somatic components of spells by using an
arcane focus, typically a wand.
