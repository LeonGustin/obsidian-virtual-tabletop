## SECTION I: THE PRIMARY ANTAGONISTS

### The King: Xal-Kul, The Ink-Stained King

**Identity:** A Daelkyr Lord of Madness imprisoned in Xoriat.

**Method:** He "edits" reality. He views the Draconic Prophecy as a manuscript and seeks to spill "living ink" across its pages to rewrite the fate of Khorvaire.

**The Connection:** He is the common thread in every PC's tragedy. He didn't just watch their lives ruin; he orchestrated the "edits" that led them to this train.

---

### The Lieutenant: The Void-Forged Horror (Formerly Hrothgrin)

**The Fall:** During a battle with a Gibbering Mouther on the Lightning Rail, the monster's warping touch cracked the Obsidian Maw.

**The Possession:** An ancient psionic entity—a shard of Xal-Kul—erupted from the hammer and possessed the Dwarf.

**The Form:** A "gory mess of a humanoid." His skin is sloughing off to reveal jagged basalt and violet psionic fire. His ribcage has split into a vertical, tooth-filled maw. He is now a Large Aberration acting as Xal-Kul's physical general on Eberron.

## SCENE 1.5: THE EVENT HORIZON OF MADNESS

*"As Hrothgrin's mutated form is reduced to 0 HP, his body doesn't collapse—it implodes. The violet psionic fire in his chest collapses into a singular, infinitely black needlepoint. The sound is like a scream played in reverse. Reality around the corpse begins to buckle, peeling away like wet paper to reveal the pulsing, impossible colors of Xoriat, the Realm of Madness."*

### The Mechanics of the Tear

**The Event:** A 40-foot radius Gravity Well centered on the spot where Hrothgrin died.

1. **The Pull:** Every creature within 40 feet must make a DC 14 Strength or Dexterity Saving Throw. On a failure, dragged 10 feet toward the Tear.
2. **The Shredding:** The car is now Difficult Terrain.
3. **The Timer:** 5 Rounds of initiative. On Round 5, the carriage disintegrates and crashes into the ravine.

### The Outcomes

**Outcome A: The Leap (Survival)**
- DC 13 Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics)
- Success: 2d6 bludgeoning, washes ashore stable
- Failure: 4d6, falls unconscious

**Outcome B: The Crash (5-Round Limit)**
- 6d6 force damage. Wake up 1 hour later stabilized by Genesis or Berrik.

**Outcome C: The Void (Falling into the Tear)**
- DC 15 Wisdom Saving Throw
- **FAIL — Gift of the Black Slumber:** Succumb to the velvet silence. Identity frays. Receive Black Slumber gift.
- **SUCCESS — Gift of the Glimpse:** You look upon Xal-Kul. A colossus of living geometry — billions of razor-sharp parchment pages bleeding black ichor. His face is a hollow void shaped like a crown. He holds a quill made from the wing of a dead angel. Receive Glimpse gift.
