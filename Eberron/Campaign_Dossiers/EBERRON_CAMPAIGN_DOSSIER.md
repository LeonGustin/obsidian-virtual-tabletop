## **SECTION I: CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW**

### **Campaign Premise**

An investigation into the disappearance of pages from the Original Draconic Prophecy, leading to a planar incursion and the discovery of Xal-Kul, the Ink-Stained King—a Daelkyr lord imprisoned in Xoriat (the Realm of Madness).

**Central BBEG:** Xal-Kul (Daelkyr Lord)

**BBEG Goal:** Reach the Vault of Whispers, tear out pages of the Original Draconic Prophecy, use them to tear open the dimensional barrier between Eberron and Xoriat, unleashing madness.

**Campaign Hook:** Every PC tragedy, every mysterious event, every player death—all connect back to Xal-Kul's machinations. This isn't coincidence. This is a planar entity systematically hunting each party member.

## **SECTION III: THE MUTANT HROTHGRIN ENCOUNTER**

### **Encounter Overview**

**Location:** Lightning Rail - Cargo Car 7  
**Trigger:** Gibbering Mouther encounter wounds Obsidian Maw  
**Result:** Psionic entity escapes hammer, bonds with Hrothgrin, forces horrific transformation  

### **The Transformation Sequence**

**Stage 1: Obsidian Maw Cracks**
- Gibbering Mouther has been attacking
- Combat ongoing
- Obsidian Maw (Hrothgrin's hammer) sustains critical damage
- Visible crack appears in obsidian surface

**Stage 2: Psionic Discharge**
- Entity imprisoned in hammer is released
- Psychic energy floods Hrothgrin's body
- Hrothgrin screams in agony (description: voice distorts, cracks, becomes inhuman)

**Stage 3: Physical Transformation**
- Hrothgrin's body mutates into Mutated Horror (CR 6 Large Aberration)
- Skin becomes translucent, showing pulsing ichor inside
- Extra limbs form (inky tendrils)
- Eyes glow with psionic light
- Size increases to Large

**Stage 4: Combat**

Hrothgrin (now Mutated Horror) immediately attacks party:
- AC 16
- HP 165
- Psionic Miasma aura (15 ft radius, DC 16 Int save or 2d8 psychic + disadvantage)
- Ink-Born Regeneration (10 HP/turn, stops if radiant damage taken)
- Actions:
  - Multiattack: 2 Ink-Stained Fists (1d8+4 slashing + 1d4 psychic) + 1 Obsidian Spike
  - Gravity Well (recharge 5-6): DC 16 Str save or pulled 20 ft + grappled
  - Psionic Scream (recharge 6): 30 ft radius, DC 16 Wis save, 4d8 psychic + stunned

**Stage 5: Optional - Save Hrothgrin's Soul**

Heroic Option (5 DC 15 checks before HP reaches 0):
- Arcana: Stabilize psionic rift
- Religion: Exorcism  
- Athletics: Rip obsidian shards
- Medicine: Anchor life force
- Insight: Reach consciousness

**Success:** Psionic entity escapes, Hrothgrin survives as unconscious NPC ally, still has wounds/trauma

**Failure:** Hrothgrin dies, body consumed by entity, becomes avatar of Xal-Kul's power

### **Mutated Hrothgrin Stat Block**

```
MUTATED HROTHGRIN
Large Aberration, chaotic evil
Armor Class 16 (aberrant hide)
Hit Points 165 (10d10 + 100)
Speed 20 ft.

STR 19 (+4) | DEX 12 (+1) | CON 22 (+6) | INT 15 (+2) | WIS 13 (+1) | CHA 8 (-1)

Saving Throws Con +8, Int +4
Skills Perception +4
Damage Resistances psychic
Condition Immunities charmed, frightened
Senses darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 14
Languages understands Dwarvish and Common, but cannot speak (only screams)
Challenge 6 (2,300 XP)

TRAITS

Psionic Miasma. Any creature that starts its turn within 15 feet of Mutated Hrothgrin must make a DC 16 Intelligence saving throw. On a failure, the creature takes 2d8 psychic damage and has disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.

Reactive Mutation. When Mutated Hrothgrin takes more than 15 damage from a single source, a shard of obsidian erupts from its body. Choose one creature within 20 feet of Mutated Hrothgrin that it can see. That creature must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d8 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. A creature that fails this save is knocked prone.

Ink-Born Regeneration. Mutated Hrothgrin regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn. If Mutated Hrothgrin takes radiant damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of its next turn. Mutated Hrothgrin's body is destroyed only if it starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn't regenerate.

ACTIONS

Multiattack. Mutated Hrothgrin makes two attacks with its Ink-Stained Fists and uses Obsidian Spike if available.

Ink-Stained Fist. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8 + 4 (5) slashing damage plus 1d4 (2) psychic damage.

Obsidian Spike. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 2d8 + 4 (13) piercing damage.

Gravity Well (Recharge 5-6). Each creature in a 20-foot radius centered on Mutated Hrothgrin must make a DC 16 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a creature is pulled up to 20 feet toward Mutated Hrothgrin and is grappled (escape DC 16). On a successful save, the creature isn't pulled or grappled.

Psionic Scream (Recharge 6). Mutated Hrothgrin emits a psychic wail. Each creature within 30 feet of it that can hear it must make a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 4d8 psychic damage and is stunned until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage and isn't stunned.

LEGENDARY ACTIONS

Mutated Hrothgrin can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Mutated Hrothgrin regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

Shift Reality. Mutated Hrothgrin magically teleports up to 20 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.

Psychic Lash. Mutated Hrothgrin targets one creature within 60 feet that it can see. The target must make a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw, taking 3d6 psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Obsidian Barrage. Mutated Hrothgrin hurls 1d3 shards of obsidian. Each shard targets one creature within 20 feet of Mutated Hrothgrin that it can see. A target must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d6 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Command Ink-Born. Mutated Hrothgrin summons 1d2 Ink-Born Skirmishers in unoccupied spaces it can see within 30 feet. They act on Mutated Hrothgrin's initiative and obey its spoken commands.
```

## **SECTION IV: THE XORIAT TEAR & ESCAPE**

### **Timeline: 4 Rounds Until Catastrophe**

**Round 1 (Manifestation):**
- Rift opens 30 feet ahead of party
- Tears space: 20 ft wide initially
- Train lurches (DC 11 Dex save or prone)
- No damage yet, but observation round shows horror ahead

**Round 2 (Psionic Shockwave):**
- Rift expands to 60 feet wide
- Front 2 cars + locomotive vanish
- All creatures within 40 ft: DC 12 Wis save or 2d6 psychic + disadvantage
- Car lurches forward (DC 11 Dex save or prone)
- **Party must escape in 2 rounds**

**Round 3 (Point of No Return):**
- Rift expands to 80 feet
- Trestle bridge collapses (DC 12 Dex save or 1d6 bludgeoning from debris)
- Creatures in car: DC 13 Str save or pulled 10 ft toward rift
- **Last chance to escape before car enters rift**

**Round 4 (The Plunge):**
- Car enters rift
- Reality inverts
- Geometry breaks
- Final escape attempts possible
- If party doesn't escape, they're pulled to Xoriat

### **Escape Options (Multiple Valid Paths)**

**Option A: Jump to Cliff Ledge (20 ft away)**
- DC 12 Acrobatics check
- Success: Land safely, 0 damage
- Failure: Fall 10 ft to lower ledge, 1d6 damage (safe)
- Critical Failure: 2d6 damage, must make DC 13 Str save to grab handrail

**Option B: Magic Teleportation**
- Misty Step, Dimension Door, Feather Fall, Levitate
- Automatic success (no saves)
- Removes from danger immediately
- Best for vulnerable party members

**Option C: Close the Rift**
- DC 15 Arcana check (hard but possible with Help action)
- Success: Rift shrinks 50%, party can ride crash for 3d6 damage
- Failure: Spell slot wasted, rift persists

**Option D: Ride the Crash (Most Cinematic)**
- DC 13 Dex save
- Success: 3d6 damage, arrive in Xoriat conscious
- Failure: 5d6 damage, arrive unconscious (stable at 1 HP)
- Landing: Corrupted island in Xoriat (not immediate danger)

**Option E: Heroic Sacrifice**
- Hold rift edge, grant allies advantage on escape
- Takes full crash (8d6 damage minimum)
- Arrives in Xoriat, heavily wounded
- Narrative: "I'll buy you time!"

## **SECTION V: MAIN ANTAGONIST PROFILE**

### **XAL-KUL, THE INK-STAINED KING**

**Entity Type:** Daelkyr Lord (Xoriatian aberration, not native to Eberron)

**Current Imprisonment:** Trapped in Xoriat (Realm of Madness), behind dimensional barrier

**Overall Goal:** Tear open the barrier between Eberron and Xoriat, unleash madness, rule both realms

**Specific Objective (This Campaign):** Acquire pages from the Original Draconic Prophecy (stored in Vault of Whispers)

**Why the Prophecy?** Unknown to party initially (reveal in later sessions)
- Prophecy contains words of power that can rupture dimensions
- Xal-Kul's true name is written there (he cannot act fully until name is spoken)
- Pages can be used in a ritual to open permanent portal
- (DM choice on exact mechanics)

### **Xal-Kul's Connection to Each PC**

**Qinmar:** Blinded by Xal-Kul (personal attack, high threat assessment)

**Azura:** Feeding false prophecies through her visions (corruption attempt)

**Karn:** Possibly caused original lycanthropic infection, wants to re-infect (control attempt)

**Scrap:** Hunting for the Vault map (highest priority, direct threat to objective)

**Liriel:** Sent Ink-Born doppelgänger to steal identity (intelligence gathering, disruption)

**Vaelrion:** May have sent/guided the Solar Entity hunting Vaelrion (external pressure, isolation attempt)

**Human Cleric:** [Unknown connection, DM chooses]

**Dwarf Assassin:** Connection through Hrothgrin's death (revenge motivation, possible leverage)

### **Xal-Kul's Influence Mechanics**

**Awareness Check (DC 15 Insight):**
- Party can sense they're being hunted
- Uncovered through: Strange coincidences, recurring NPCs, too-perfect timing of threats
- Realization: "This is not random. Someone is orchestrating this."

**Psychic Pressure (At-Will):**
- Once per day per PC, Xal-Kul can impose disadvantage on a save/attack
- Manifests as overwhelming dread, dark thoughts, whispers
- Party doesn't know it's Xal-Kul at first

**Mark of Xal-Kul (Plot Device):**
- Each PC who encounters Xal-Kul's direct influence gains an invisible mark
- Multiple marks = stronger connection = more influence
- When party gathers (like on the train), marks resonate, causing shared nightmare/vision

### **WHAT-IF #3: Party Fails to Escape Before Round 4**

**Outcome:**
- Some or all PCs pulled to Xoriat
- Those who escape are stranded in Eberron (railyard, no way home)
- Those in Xoriat are trapped, facing alien world

**Campaign Branches:**
- **Branch A:** Rescue Mission (stranded PCs go to Vault of Whispers to find allies, portal home)
- **Branch B:** Two-Front Campaign (stranded PCs form new party, invaded PCs become "lost" until midway point)
- **Branch C:** Xoriat Exploration (PCs must navigate alien world, discover Vault of Whispers is real, approach from inside)

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Stranded PCs take 1-3 levels of exhaustion
- Xoriat gravity/reality effects cause ongoing damage (DC 12 Con save, 1d4 psychic)
- Resources are scarce (no healing potions, food, water)
- Time pressure: Portal closes in X days

- Inquisitor suspects party caused the crash (intentionally or not)
- Possible combat or negotiation

**Outcome:**

- Party must protect Scrap at all costs

- Shifts party dynamic
- Scrap becomes target #1
- Party may split (some protect Scrap, some fight)
- Creates moral dilemma: "Do we risk Scrap to defeat Xal-Kul?"

### **WHAT-IF #9: Party Reaches Vault of Whispers**

**Outcome:**
- Party enters the massive obsidian ziggurat
- Seven-tier structure, each tier contains horrors
- Xal-Kul's presence is overwhelming (psychic pressure, constant saves)
- Party must navigate to inner sanctum where Draconic Prophecy pages are kept

**Each Tier Challenge (Scaling):**
- Tier 1: Cultists of Xal-Kul (combat)
- Tier 2: Crystalline maze (navigation, DC 13 saves)
- Tier 3: Aberrations (combat)
- Tier 4: Mind-bending puzzle (Insight/Arcana checks)
- Tier 5: Xoriatian constructs (combat, high damage)
- Tier 6: Psychic assault (mass Wis saves, 3d8 psychic each)
- Tier 7: Inner sanctum (Xal-Kul encounter or final puzzle)

**Campaign Culmination:**
- Party must decide: Destroy Prophecy pages, steal them, or bargain with Xal-Kul
- Each choice leads to different endgame

**Outcome:**

**Consequences:**
- Betrayed party members become enemies
- Party may be split (some accept, some refuse)

## **SECTION XI: FINAL NOTES & CAMPAIGN PHILOSOPHY**

### **Tone & Theme**

- **Cosmic Horror:** Xal-Kul is not fully understandable, his motives are alien
- **Personal Tragedy:** Each PC's tragedy connects to the main plot
- **Body Horror:** Hrothgrin's transformation is disturbing and memorable
- **Moral Gray:** Not all choices are clear, some require sacrifice

### **DM Guidance**

- Never fully explain Xal-Kul's motives (keep it mysterious)
- Use fear of the unknown (off-screen threats)
- Make the Vault feel ancient and impossibly large
- Emphasize environmental storytelling (ruins, corruption, wrongness)
- Allow PCs to feel like they're racing against time

### **Player Agency**

- Multiple escape routes from Train crash
- Multiple ways to approach Vault
- Multiple ending paths (defeat, compromise, escape, sacrifice)
- No single "correct" choice
