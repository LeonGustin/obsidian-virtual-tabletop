   Tatters of the Titan Scroll

Sharn, City of Towers, hides secrets aplenty amidst its wonders and
  splendor, and sometimes those secrets crawl back to the surface.
 A strange summons sends a party of heroes on the search for rem-
nants of a stone-like scroll broken up long ago, but others also seek
the prize. Finding the Titan Scroll puts the characters in the path of
          danger, action, excitement, and towards a greater
                      mystery waiting to unfold.

              A 6-8 hour adventure for Tier 1 (Levels 1-4) characters
                              Optimized for APL 1
